Effects

TouchUp brings creative photo editing to iPad, enabling anyone to make common adjustments and create captivating art with their photos.

TouchUp has over a dozen effects which can be applied in layers to your photo.

Tap + Slide = Brush

Any effect in TouchUp can be brushed off or on with a tap and slide of your finger. You can express your creative side or make fine-tuned modifications for the perfect look in no time.

Until you try it, you won't believe how much fun it is to brush directly on your photos.

Easy to Use

You don't have to be a pro to make something beautiful with TouchUp. Watch our tutorials above to see how anyone can easily create stunning results.

If you are a pro, we have you covered with advanced effects like Dodge and Burn.

Import / Export

TouchUp lets you import photos from your Photo Library. You can also take a dip in Flickr's photo stream for new images to play with, or with a Flickr account, you can import your own photos.

When you are done, you can share your masterpieces quickly by email, Facebook, Twitter, Flickr or even print our your creations using AirPrint.

Showcase, Shake, and Share your photos


RoseGlobe lets you admire your sweetheart in an all new way. Place your favorite picture in the elegant globe and enjoy as the decorative hearts dance around. Tilt, shake and move your phone and watch the globe react just like a real romance-themed snow globe.

If you like you can take a snapshot of the globe and share it on Facebook, Twitter or by email. Its the perfect way to send a special thought to your valentine or loved one.
Realistic

Confetti hearts in the globe react to gravity and motion as you twist, turn and shake your phone

Photos

Customize the scene with photos from your library or use the camera on your iPhone to take a new photo.

Share

Send a picture of your special scene by email, Facebook and Twitter.

Let it snow!

Flick the main screen sideways to switch to a traditional snow globe with snowflakes instead of hearts. (Version 2.1)

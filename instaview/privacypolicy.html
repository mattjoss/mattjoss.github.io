RogueSheep Instaview Privacy Statement


Protecting your privacy is important to us. We hope the following information will help you understand how RogueSheep uses and safeguards your personal information when you use Instaview.

Instaview does not collect usage data. Your Instagram username and password are sent to Instagram's servers using the xauth authentication method. After successful authentication a token is stored on your device which allows use of the Instagram APIs. This token is stored on your device after you quit Instaview to allow automatic login when you relaunch the application. To remove this token, choose the Logout item from the main Instaview menu. All images and related data are obtained using Instagram's APIs and stored locally on your device. None of your personal information is ever stored on any RogueSheep servers. Images will routinely be cached on your local machine to allow for optimum performance of the application.

From time to time, we may revise the policy as stated here. If you would like to receive a copy of our most current privacy policy please send an email to support.instaview@roguesheep.com.

Share life on the go.


Postage for the iPhone allows you to easily create beautiful electronic postcards from your photos and share them with your friends and family. Whether you are traveling, celebrating a holiday, announcing an important life-event or just having fun with your photos, Postage allows you to keep in touch while you are on the go.

Beautiful Templates

With over 90 unique postcard designs, you can find the perfect postcard for a multitude of situations. Need to send a Mother's Day, Father's Day, birthday or holiday greeting? Postage has postcards for all those occasions and more. Perhaps you just want a nice frame to give your photo a bit more style. Postage has you covered there as well. From simple letter templates to cutout templates, Postage has a template to fit your needs.

See our gallery of templates
Quick and Easy

Postage makes it easy to create great postcards in just a few steps. Pick a template, add a photo and some text and you're ready to share. Postage walks you through the process with beautiful animations and applies your actions directly to the postcard so you can always see how it looks.

Personalize

Postage offers many ways to make your postcard your own. Pinch to zoom and rotate your photo to focus on the most important area of your photo. Apply one of ten image effects such as Black and White, Sepia or Brighten to get that perfect look for your photo. You can also change the font, style and color of your message to create a look that is just right for your postcard.

Share Your Creation

Send your postcard by email, share on Facebook or post to Twitter. Save it to your photo library or Copy and Paste it to an MMS message.

Themed Apps

For the budget-conscious we also offer three smaller applications centered around specific themes: Halloween, Holiday and Romance. These each have a single category of postcard designs. Our main application contains every template offered in these smaller versions and many more.
